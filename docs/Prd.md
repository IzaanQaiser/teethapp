# Pearly — V1 Product Requirements Document (PRD)

## 1. Product Overview

**Product Name:** Pearly  
**Platform:** iOS (SwiftUI)  
**Version:** v1  
**Storage:** Local-only  
**Accounts:** None  

Pearly helps users estimate their baseline tooth shade, track relative whitening progress over time, and follow a personalized, realistic whitening plan.

This product is not a medical or dental device.

---

## 2. Product Goals

### Primary Goal
Provide users with a **trustworthy baseline**, a **clear sense of progress**, and a **structured daily plan** that motivates consistency.

### Secondary Goals
- Build user trust through transparency and privacy
- Create a natural premium conversion point
- Avoid hype, fake certainty, or scammy UX patterns

---

## 3. Non-Goals (Explicit)

- No medical diagnosis or professional advice
- No guaranteed whitening claims
- No accounts or cloud sync
- No cross-device restore
- No advanced AI explainability in v1

---

## 4. Target User

- Age: 18–35
- Appearance-conscious
- Drinks coffee/tea or has mild staining
- Skeptical of “AI” marketing
- Wants fast value with low friction

---

## 5. Core Constraints (Non-Negotiable)

- User must be 18+
- Photos never leave the device
- All progress is relative (baseline → trend)
- Lighting and camera quality affect accuracy
- Calm, premium, non-clinical tone
- No login required in v1

---

## 6. Monetization (V1)

- Free trial available
- Paywall appears **after**:
  - baseline scan
  - results
  - personalized plan preview
- Free users:
  - can access Day 1 of the plan
- Premium unlocks:
  - full plan (Day 4+)
  - long-term progress tracking
  - advanced insights (conceptual only in v1)

---

## 7. Success Criteria

V1 is successful if:
- Users complete baseline → plan flow
- Day 1 plan feels actionable
- Progress screen feels meaningful within 7 days
- App feels private, calm, and trustworthy
