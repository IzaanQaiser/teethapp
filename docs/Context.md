**App:** pearly
**Platform:** iOS, SwiftUI
**Storage:** Local-only
**Accounts:** None
**Photos:** On-device only

**Goal:**
Estimate baseline tooth shade, track **relative improvement over time**, and guide users with a **personalized, realistic whitening plan**.

**Constraints:**

* Not a medical device
* No guaranteed results
* Lighting affects accuracy
* No cloud, no accounts
* Calm, trustworthy tone

**Final v1 Flow (locked):**

1. Welcome
2. Trust & Consent (privacy, disclaimer, 18+)
3. How It Works (3 steps)
4. Baseline Capture → Scan Animation
5. Results (baseline + confidence)
6. Personalized Plan (preview)
7. Paywall (free trial, small X)
8. Plan View (Day 1 open; Day 2–3 collapsed; Day 4+ locked)
9. Post-Decision Reassurance
10. Rescan Framing

**Design Notes:**

* UI/UX skeleton only
* No backend logic
* Focus on hierarchy, clarity, trust
* No gimmicks, no hype
