# Pearly — V1 Screens Specification

This document defines all screens in Pearly v1, their purpose, and required elements.
No backend or logic is specified here.

---

## 1. Welcome Screen

**Purpose:** First impression + value proposition

Elements:
- App name: Pearly
- One-line value statement
- Primary CTA: “Get started”

---

## 2. Trust & Consent Screen

**Purpose:** Establish trust and obtain confirmation

Elements:
- Bullet points:
  - Not a medical device
  - Results vary
  - Photos stay on device
  - Lighting affects accuracy
- Checkbox:
  - “I am 18 years or older”
- CTA: “I understand & continue”

---

## 3. How It Works Screen

**Purpose:** Explain process clearly

Elements:
- 3-step visual explanation:
  1. Take a baseline photo
  2. Lighting is normalized and shade is estimated
  3. Track relative progress over time
- Short captions
- CTA: “Start my scan”

---

## 4. Baseline Capture Screen

**Purpose:** Capture usable baseline images

Elements:
- Live camera preview
- Mouth framing guide
- Lighting quality indicator
- Instructions (no filters, neutral lighting)
- Capture flow:
  - 3 images (front, slight left, slight right)

---

## 5. Scan / Analyzing Screen

**Purpose:** Build confidence during processing

Elements:
- Progress animation
- Status messages:
  - Normalizing lighting
  - Estimating baseline
  - Preparing your plan

---

## 6. Results Screen

**Purpose:** Deliver first value moment

Elements:
- Estimated baseline shade (simple scale)
- Confidence indicator
- “What this means” explanation
- Disclaimer reminder
- CTA: “View my plan”

---

## 7. Personalized Plan Preview Screen

**Purpose:** Create hope and intent

Elements:
- Summary of inputs:
  - Goal
  - Sensitivity
  - Baseline
- Plan overview:
  - Daily habits
  - Weekly habits
  - Avoid list
- Time-per-day estimate
- CTA: “Unlock my full plan”

---

## 8. Paywall Screen

**Purpose:** Convert to premium

Elements:
- Headline focused on unlocking progress
- Free trial mention
- Premium benefits list
- Primary CTA: “Start free trial”
- Secondary action: small close (X)

---

## 9. Plan Screen

**Purpose:** Drive daily engagement

Elements:
- Day 1:
  - Expanded
  - Full checklist
- Day 2–3:
  - Collapsed previews
- Day 4+:
  - Locked
  - Shows what will unlock with premium

---

## 10. Post-Decision Reassurance Screen

**Purpose:** Reduce anxiety and refunds

Elements:
- Cancel anytime message
- Privacy reassurance
- Consistency encouragement

---

## 11. Rescan Framing Screen

**Purpose:** Set retention expectation

Elements:
- When to rescan (weekly)
- Why consistency matters
- CTA: “Got it”
